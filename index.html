You're Not Crazy. You're in Perimenopause.

Most women don't recognize the signs. Here's what perimenopause actually looks like:

🌡️

Hot Flashes & Night Sweats

Sudden heat waves that disrupt your day and ruin your sleep

😴

Sleep Disruption

Waking up at 3am, unable to fall back asleep

🧠

Brain Fog

Forgetting words, losing focus, feeling mentally "off"

😰

Mood Swings & Anxiety

Irritability, sadness, or panic attacks that come out of nowhere

📅

Irregular Periods

Cycles that are suddenly shorter, longer, or unpredictable

⚡

Fatigue & Low Energy

Exhausted no matter how much you rest

The average woman suffers for 4 years before getting help. Most doctors dismiss these symptoms as "just stress" or "part of aging." But you deserve answers.

We Start Earlier

Most menopause platforms focus on full menopause (12 months without a period). We focus on perimenopause — the 4-10 years leading up to it — when symptoms start but most women don't know what's happening.

If you're 35-50 and experiencing unexplained symptoms, you're in the right place.

Age 35-40

Early perimenopause symptoms start (often dismissed as stress)

Age 40-45

Symptoms intensify (irregular periods, mood changes, sleep issues)

Age 45-50

Late perimenopause (hot flashes, night sweats, brain fog peak)

Age 51+

Menopause (12 months without a period)
